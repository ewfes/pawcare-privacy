Privacy Policy — PawCare

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the PawCare mobile application (package com.pawpilot.app, the “App”) handles your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Summary

PawCare does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App works fully offline. All information you enter (such as details about your pets, reminders, notes, and settings) is stored only on your device and never leaves it.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not include analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and makes no network requests to our servers or any third party.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Any data you create in the App (pet profiles, schedules, reminders, photos you add, preferences) is saved locally in the App’s own storage on your device. We have no access to this data. If you uninstall the App or use the “Clear data” option, this information is permanently removed.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions

The App offers optional premium subscriptions (premium_monthly, premium_yearly). Payments are processed entirely by Google Play. We never receive or store your payment details. Google’s handling of payment data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

The App may request access to features such as notifications or your photo library only to provide its functionality (e.g. showing reminders or letting you attach a pet photo). These are used locally and are not transmitted anywhere.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected or transmitted, no personal information of children is processed.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
